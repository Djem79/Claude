## Positioning (don't copy-paste — vary the opening)

Use the boilerplates from [outreach-templates](./outreach-templates-2026-06.md):

- **Business / local directories** → lead with credibility + service ("Dubai property advisory for international investors").
- **Expat directories** → lead with audience fit ("helping expats and overseas buyers navigate the UAE market").
- Keep the **NAP byte-identical everywhere** — Rasis Business Centre - 5, 5th Floor, Office 502 (EBC03), Al Barsha 1, Dubai · +971 50 696 0435 · info@worldwise.pro. Citation consistency is the point.

## Don't

- Don't pay for bulk "submit to 500 directories" services — spam, dilutes the profile.
- Don't submit to DR<10 junk directories (Google spam detection penalises them).
- Don't vary the NAP between listings.
